## Pluto's Small Moons

**Styx**
1. Smallest of Pluto's known moons
2. Innermost of Pluto's known moons (i.e., closer to Pluto than Nix?)
3. Orbits between Charon and Nix

**Nix**
1. Small, elongated moon
2. Chaotic rotation

**Kerberos**
1. Pluto's second-smallest moon
2. Double-lobed shape

**Hydra**
1. Outermost known moon of Pluto
2. Elongated shape
3. Highly reflective, icy surface

## Haumea System

**Hi'iaka**
1. Haumea's outer moon (not inner)
2. Period: 49 days
3. Diameter ~310 km
4. Named for Hawaiian goddess

**Namaka**
1. Haumea's inner moon (not outer)
2. Period: 18 days
3. Diameter ~170 km
4. Orbit perturbed by Hi'iaka

## Makemake System

**Makemake**
1. Second-brightest KBO after Pluto
2. Has one known moon (MK2)

**MK2**
1. Designation: S/2015 (136472) 1
2. Period: 18.0 days
3. Distance: ~22,250 km from Makemake
4. Orbit edge-on to Earth
5. Very dark surface (~4% reflectivity)
6. Diameter ~175 km

## Leleakuhonua

**Leleakuhonua (2015 TG387)**
1. Discovered in 2015
2. One of the largest known semi-major axes at ~1,090 AU
3. One of four confirmed members of the Sednoid class

## Chariklo

**Chariklo (1997 CU26)**
1. Largest known centaur
2. Orbits the Sun between Jupiter and Neptune
3. Average diameter ~250 km
4. Surface composed of water ice, silicate minerals, and organic compounds
5. First minor (non-dwarf) planet discovered to have rings
6. Rings discovered in 2013 during a stellar occultation
7. Inner ring named Oiapoque, ~7 km wide
8. Outer ring named Chui, ~3 km wide
9. Two rings separated by a 9-km gap
10. Rings orbit at ~400 km from Chariklo's center

---

## Orcus System

**Orcus (2004 DW)**
1. Aphelion reached in 2019 at ~48 AU
2. Perihelion: ~1895 and next ~2142 at ~30 AU
3. Orbital period: 247 years
4. Diameter: ~910 km
5. Discovered Feb 17, 2004 by Brown, Trujillo, and Rabinowitz

**Vanth**
1. Mass ratio: 16% of Orcus (higher than Charon/Pluto at 12%)
2. Total separation: 9,000 km
3. Vanth orbits barycenter at ~7,770 km
4. Orcus orbits barycenter at ~1,230 km
5. Period: 9.54 days
6. Eccentricity: ~0.007
7. Inclination: ~90 deg to ecliptic (nearly face-on from Earth)
8. ALMA 2016 resolved the barycentric orbital motion directly
9. Discovered Nov 2005 by Brown in Hubble images, announced Feb 2007
10. Diameter: ~443 km (from 2017 occultation)

## Xiangliu (Gonggong's moon)

**Xiangliu**
1. Gonggong's only known moon
2. Named after a nine-headed serpent minister in Chinese mythology
3. Discovered 2016 by Kiss et al. in Hubble images from 2010
4. Period: 25.22 days
5. Distance: ~24,000 km
6. Diameter: ~100 km (albedo-dependent estimate)
7. Highly eccentric orbit (e~0.29)
8. Inclined ~83 deg to ecliptic (nearly pole-on from Earth)
