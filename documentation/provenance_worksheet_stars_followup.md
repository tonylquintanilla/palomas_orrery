## Bellatrix (gam Ori)
1. Third brightest in Orion, 27th brightest in entire night sky
2. Spectral type B2 III (blue giant)
3. Surface temperature ~21,500 K
4. Luminosity ~6,400 times the Sun
5. Expected to end life as a massive white dwarf

## Mintaka (del Ori)
1. Multiple star system of six or more stars
2. Main component is triple system (Aa1, Aa2, Ab)
3. Aa1/Aa2 form an eclipsing binary
4. Spectral type O9.5 II (hot blue giants)
5. Distance ~900 light-years

## Alnilam (eps Ori)
1. Fourth brightest in Orion, 29th brightest in the night sky
2. Spectral type B0 Ia (blue supergiant)
3. Distance ~2,000 light-years
4. Luminosity ~375,000 times the Sun
5. Surrounded by reflection nebula NGC 1990

## Alnitak (zet Ori)
1. Triple star system (Alnitak Aa, Ab, B)
2. Primary (Aa) spectral type O9.7 Ibe (blue supergiant)
3. Surrounded by Flame Nebula NGC 2024
4. Some evidence Aa/Ab may form an eclipsing binary

## Naos (zet Pup)
1. Apparent magnitude 2.25, brightest in Puppis
2. Mass ~22.5 times the Sun
3. Surface temperature ~42,000 K (O-type)
4. Runaway star -- ejected when binary companion went supernova
5. Rotational velocity ~220 km/s
