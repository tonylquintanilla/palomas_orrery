| F4 | Composition | "primarily nitrogen and oxygen" |
| F5 | Temperature at sea level | "about 15 degC (59 degF)" |
| F6 | Temperature at stratopause | "-60 degC (-76 degF)" |

Note on F6: The stratopause temperature is actually near 0 degC
(the stratosphere warms with altitude due to ozone absorption).
The TROPOPAUSE is around -60 degC. Please verify which boundary
is meant and whether the temperature is correct for it.

Likely source: NOAA, NASA Earth Fact Sheet.
