## What We Added

### New Section: "The Discovery Pathway"

**Location:** After "Discovery Over Delivery" section

**Key Concepts:**

1. **Alignment itself reveals solutions**
   - Not just executing what's asked
   - But discovering what's actually needed
   - Through the conversation itself

2. **The rhythm of discovery**
   - Not: `Computer do this → [0.003 sec] → Done`
   - But: `Problem → Options → Align → Work → Review → Discover → Repeat`
   - Why this takes hours (and that's GOOD!)

3. **Why conversation enables discovery**
   - Externalizes thinking
   - Surfaces assumptions
   - Enables reflection
   - Builds on ideas
   - Allows course correction

4. **The chatbot advantage**
   - "Chatbot" form factor is OPTIMAL for complex creative work
   - Not a toy or inefficient interface
   - But the best medium for discovery
   - Conversation IS the discovery mechanism

5. **Alignment requires conversation**
   - Can't align through coercion
   - Can't align through assumption
   - Can't align through instant response
   - **Must align through dialog**
