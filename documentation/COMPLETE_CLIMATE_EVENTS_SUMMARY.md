## What Was Added

Three major Holocene climate events with profound human impacts:

### 1. Younger Dryas (12,900-11,700 years ago) ⭐ NEW!
**Color**: Dark turquoise (#00CED1)  
**Position**: ~12,300 years ago  
**Arrow**: Points DOWN (sudden cooling)

**The Story:**
- Sudden return to Ice Age conditions in DECADES
- ~10°C temperature drop
- Meltwater disrupted Gulf Stream
- Megafauna extinctions (mammoths, saber-tooths)
- **Forced humans to invent agriculture**
- Ended abruptly → Holocene begins

**Why it matters:** Shows climate can flip FAST. Agriculture was born from this crisis!

---

### 2. Medieval Warm Period (950-1250 CE)
**Color**: Dark orange (#FF8C00)  
**Position**: ~1100 CE  
**Arrow**: Points UP (warming)

**The Story:**
- Warmest period of last 2,000 years
- Vikings settled Greenland
- European prosperity, population growth
- ~0.3°C warmer than pre-industrial

**Why it matters:** Natural variability baseline - shows climate changes without human forcing.

### 3. Little Ice Age (1300-1850 CE)
**Color**: Royal blue (#4169E1)

**The Story:**

- **Viking Greenland colonies abandoned**
